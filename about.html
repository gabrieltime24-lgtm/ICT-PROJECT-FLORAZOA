About This Website

FLORAZOA is an educational platform created for Grade 11 students to explore the world of plants (flora) and animals (fauna). The website provides interactive content, modules, references, and quizzes to enhance learning in biology.

Objectives

  • Provide curriculum-aligned learning materials for plants and animals.
  • Encourage self-directed learning through quizzes and resources.
  • Promote understanding of biodiversity and ecological importance.
  • Offer a user-friendly interface for easy navigation of flora and fauna topics.

Who Can Use This Website?

This platform is designed for:

  • Grade 11 students studying biology.
  • Teachers seeking online teaching resources.
  • Anyone interested in learning about plants and animals.
